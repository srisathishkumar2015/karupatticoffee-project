Steps to START our NELLAI KARUPATTI COFFEE franchise:

  • Search or Confirm Location to start our Brand.
  • Make site visit payment to proceed for the next step.
  • After visiting the booked location, the quotation finalization will take place
  • Advance payment should be made to hold the Location confirmed as per terms.
  • After the franchise amount is paid in full, franchise agreement is signed by both parties.
  • All required equipment will be sent before the opening of the branch, to the location.
  • Training will be given to the workers and also the franchise owner.
  • Inventory management procurement details will also be provided.
