About Nellai Karupatti Coffee


To ‘Serve Healthy’ is our priority and ‘Your Satisfaction’ is our aim.


NELLAI KARUPATTI COFFEE is an authentic coffee outlet. We are known for our signature Karupatti coffee and other Karupatti infused snacks, which are both traditional and tasty. To "Serve Healthy" is our priority and "Your Satisfaction" is our aim. We are committed to bring back"OUR VISION" of serving healthy, organic food and re-introducing the traditional food system in this dawn of modern era, with our authentic food infused with innovation.
